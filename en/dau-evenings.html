Interestingly, “Dau Evenings” holds evening events on the campus of Phystech (MIPT), Russia's leading technical institute. Dau is the nickname for the great physicist and scientist Landau. When designing the event, we tried to capture the rich history of MIPT, the enthusiasm of students, and the comforting atmosphere of the quizzes.

Initially, the design for "Dau Nights" looked very different to me. However, at Sputnik, we always develop concepts in a thorough and comprehensive manner. While searching for inspiration, I came across an intentionally careless drawing of a bird. Thisinaccuracy in the original idea led me to believe that it would fit well with the student concept for “Dau Nights.” Once I had an idea, it was just a matter of bringing it to fruition.

— Alexander Malevich, CDO Sputnik Solutions

Nobel laureate L. D. Landau - or Dau, as he was known - has studied large-scale and complex problems in physics throughout his life. Surprisingly, he is known not only for his scientific work, but also for his sense of humor. Landau's non-scientific theory was the theory of happiness, which he believed everyone should strive for. The “Dow Nights”, a combination of fun and seriousness, reflects this philosophy, and we have placed the great scientist at the center of our logo.

The next step was to decide on the color scheme for the project. I chose muted red and gray tones. Red symbolizes the setting sun and gray harmonizes perfectly with it. The combination of these colors creates a cozy atmosphere and a successful design for “Dau Nights”.

— Alexander Malevich, CDO Sputnik Solutions
