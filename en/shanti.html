An old German proverb says, “If your book doesn't have an index, it's not a book.” “If your company doesn't have a website, it's not a company,” we say. Shanti, a chemical industry company, contacted Sputnik Solutions to create a website and presentation.

The first task we faced was choosing colors for the website and presentation. Sputnik Solutions' team analyzed the identity, history and advantages of the company. It turned out that “Shanti” from Sanskrit means “calmness”, “harmony”, “freedom”. The word “freedom” is symbolic, because Shanti's production capabilities allow for the implementation of in-house unique developments.

The word "freedom" turned out to be decisive when choosing the dominant colors of the website: they became white and blue. They are associated with reliability, originality, peace and masculinity. The Montserrat font family used also refers to freedom and determination at the same time.

— Vasily Pislyakov, co-founder of Sputnik Solutions
