How did it all start?

History of Sputnik Solutions

On October 4, 1957, with the roar of R-7 engines, humankind entered the space age: Soviet scientists launched the world's first artificial Earth satellite. After this great event, the set expression “Sputnik moment” even appeared in the English language, denoting the moment when society realizes the need for a new technology breakthrough. In 2011, Barack Obama, calling for an increase in the number of studies conducted, would say in his address to the nation: “This is our generation's Sputnik moment”.

We, the founders of Sputnik Solutions, were inspired by space and the people who made space closer. As young students of the best technical universities of Russia, we realized that a breakthrough was needed in the web studio market: the industry had turned into a conveyor of same-type, outdated solutions. Sputnik Solutions are designed to be this breakthrough. Our absolute advantage is our values: freedom and trust. We are against the phrase “it's not personal, it's just business”, because for Sputnik Solutions, IT solutions are more than business. So it happened that humankind has entered the era of neural networks and artificial intelligence. Sputnik Solutions products keep pace with the times: we are ready to integrate the boldest data science and machine learning solutions. Therefore, our third value is scale.

Freedom, trust, scale — this is our personal Sputnik moment. Created by breakthrough and created for breakthroughs, we are Sputnik Solutions.
