Maybe you didn't know Art-Print, but Art-Print knows you for sure: this printing house has been functioning in the label production market since 2001, their products are used on Haier, LG, BEKO electronics and IKEA furniture. Art-Print already had a well-formed identity, but there was no modern website. The company contacted Sputnik Solutions to create it.

Our team started working on the project with a thorough analysis of the customer's needs and goals. We held a series of meetings with the management of the printing house to understand their needs, target audience and expectations from the new website. This helped us create a clear action plan and define the architecture of the solution.
