Aide to the Governor

What shall you do if the snow has not been removed from your yard? There is no hotline, and appeal processing on the Dobrodel and government services portals can take a month. The founders of the All-Russia Society of Inventors and Efficiency Experts contacted Sputnik Solutions to create a modern solution to this problem.

In the 21st century, according to many experts, cities compete with each other for people. In such circumstances, it is very important for the city authorities to set up a fast and high-quality communication system with locals in order to promptly solve problems and consider proposals.
Now, to solve the problem of your city, all it takes is a call to the Aide at 8 (800) 101-24-09. They recognize and classify the request, if necessary, they will ask you to complete it. The request is processed using artificial intelligence integrated into the system.

The Aide to the Governor is an innovative solution based on artificial intelligence, which significantly speeds up and simplifies the processing of appeals from citizens to government agencies. The Aide will listen to the problem, ask clarifying questions if necessary, and then automatically determine the scope of the appeal — housing and utilities, health, social services, etc. — and send a request to the appropriate government agency. The use of AI will make it easier for authorities to learn about the problems of the population and solve them more quickly.

— Dmitry Dergunov, co-founder Sputnik Solutions
